About Me

I am PhD(y)ing at the University Jean Monnet in Saint-Etienne, working on sports injury risk reduction with a focus on Athletics (Track and Field). My work focuses on how to integrate Education, Exercise programmes, Psychological techniques and Artificial Intelligence to successfully reduce the athletes' injury risk.
